🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 July 2026 · Rheoa RPG

At Rheoa RPG (“we”, “us”, or “our”), we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal data when you use our platform – no matter whether you are in London, California, Mumbai, or anywhere else in the world.

We are committed to being transparent about our data practices and to giving you meaningful control over your information. Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with any part of it, please do not use Rheoa.

1. Data We Collect

We collect different types of data to provide, improve, and secure our service:

Category What We Collect Why
Account Data Username, email address, authentication ID (from Google OAuth), account creation date. To create and manage your account, authenticate you, and communicate with you.
Profile Data Bio, location (city/country), gender, avatar, display preferences (theme, font). To personalise your profile and your experience on the platform.
Game Data Level, XP, cash, inventory, crime history, job history, PvP stats, gang membership, property ownership, vehicle garage, race history, and achievements. To power your gameplay, track your progress, and enable game mechanics.
Device & Usage Data IP address, browser type, operating system, device type, screen resolution, network connection, referrer URL, and pages visited. To analyse usage, improve performance, detect fraud, and enforce security.
Social & Chat Data Forum posts, comments, chat messages (global and direct), friend lists, testimonials, and reactions. To enable social interactions and community features.
Cookies & Local Storage Session tokens, preference cookies (theme, font), and consent flags. To keep you logged in, remember your settings, and comply with legal requirements.
🔍 Important: We collect IP addresses and device information via ipinfo.io and UAParser.js to approximate your location and understand usage patterns. This data is anonymised where possible and never sold to third parties.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data only for the following purposes:

  • Providing the Service: To operate, maintain, and improve Rheoa, including game mechanics, chat, forums, and the freelance marketplace.
  • Personalisation: To remember your preferences (theme, font, privacy settings) and tailor content to you.
  • Communication: To send you important updates (e.g., policy changes, security alerts, achievement unlocks).
  • Moderation & Safety: To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, harassment, and violations of our Terms of Service.
  • Analytics: To understand how players interact with Rheoa, so we can improve performance and user experience.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal requests.

3. Legal Basis (GDPR & Global)

We process your data only when we have a lawful basis to do so. Depending on your jurisdiction, this includes:

  • Consent: When you explicitly consent to cookies, marketing, or data sharing (you can withdraw consent at any time).
  • Contract: To perform our contract with you (i.e., to provide the game and associated services).
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our platform, prevent fraud, and ensure security, provided these interests do not override your rights.
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with legal requirements (e.g., record‑keeping, responding to law enforcement).

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We never sell your personal data. We do share it with trusted third‑party service providers who help us run Rheoa:

  • Supabase: Our backend infrastructure provider. Your account data, game state, and messages are stored on Supabase servers (located in the US/EU).
  • Google OAuth: If you log in with Google, Google shares your email address and basic profile information with us (subject to Google’s privacy policy).
  • ipinfo.io: Used to derive your approximate location from your IP address for analytics and regional features. We do not store your full IP address indefinitely.
  • Affiliate Networks: When you click “Collect Now” on Hot Wheels products, the affiliate network receives a referrer token to track purchases (at no extra cost to you).
  • Law Enforcement: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, or to protect the safety and rights of our users and the public.

5. International Data Transfers

Rheoa is a global platform. Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your own, including the United States and the European Union.

When we transfer data internationally, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place, such as:

  • EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) with our data processors (e.g., Supabase).
  • Adherence to the UK GDPR and other applicable frameworks.
  • Relying on your explicit consent for transfers where required.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights over your personal data. We are committed to helping you exercise them:

If you are in the EU/UK (GDPR)

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure (“Right to be Forgotten”): Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Right to Restriction: Limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a machine‑readable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests (e.g., direct marketing).
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: If you have given consent (e.g., for cookies), you can withdraw it at any time.

If you are in California (CCPA/CPRA)

  • Right to Know: Request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal data we have collected.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal data (with exceptions).
  • Right to Opt‑Out: We do not sell your data, so there is nothing to opt out of – but you may still request to limit the use of your sensitive personal information.
  • Right to Non‑Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

For all users (Global)

  • Access & Corrections: You can view and edit most of your profile data directly in the game (via the Profile and Customization pages).
  • Deletion: To request account deletion, please contact us at privacy@rheoa.com.
  • Complaints: You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (e.g., ICO in the UK, CNIL in France, or the relevant authority in your jurisdiction).
📧 Exercising Your Rights: To submit a request, email us at privacy@rheoa.com. We will respond within 30 days as required by law. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy:

  • Active Accounts: Your data is retained for the duration of your account’s activity.
  • Inactive Accounts: If your account is inactive for more than 12 months, we may archive your data.
  • Account Deletion: When you delete your account, we will permanently erase your personal data within 30 days, except for anonymised usage statistics or data required for legal compliance (e.g., fraud records).
  • Cookies: Preference and analytics cookies are retained for up to 12 months from your last visit.

8. Security Measures

We implement industry‑standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL.
  • Access Control: Only essential personnel have access to production databases, and all access is logged and audited.
  • Secure Backend: Supabase provides a robust, SOC‑2 compliant infrastructure with built‑in security features (row‑level security, authentication).
  • Data Minimisation: We collect only the data we need and anonymise it wherever possible.

While we strive to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

9. Children’s Privacy

Rheoa is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have inadvertently collected data from your child, please contact us so we can delete it.

For users aged 13–18, we require parental or legal guardian consent as outlined in our Terms of Service.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes via:

  • In‑game notifications on the dashboard,
  • A prominent notice on our website, or
  • Email (if you have provided one).

The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will always reflect the latest revision. Your continued use of Rheoa after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact our Data Protection Officer (DPO) or support team:

  • General Support: support@rheoa.com
  • Privacy Requests: privacy@rheoa.com
  • In‑game: Contact a Moderator via the chat system, or use the Secret Admin Panel (Founder tier).

Thank you for trusting us with your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and building a safe, enjoyable community for everyone. 🎮
