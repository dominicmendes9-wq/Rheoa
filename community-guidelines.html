📋 Community Guidelines

Last updated: 13 July 2026 · Rheoa RPG

Welcome to Rheoa! We are building a community where players from all over the world can have fun, compete, and connect. These Community Guidelines exist to ensure that everyone feels safe, respected, and valued – no matter who they are or where they come from.

By using Rheoa (including chat, forums, gangs, and all other features), you agree to follow these guidelines. They work alongside our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

⚖️ Golden Rule: Treat others the way you would like to be treated. Rheoa is a global community – be kind, be fair, and think before you post or chat.

1. Respect & Inclusivity

We welcome players of all backgrounds, cultures, and identities. We do not tolerate behaviour that harasses, excludes, or discriminates against others.

  • Be respectful: Disagree respectfully. Personal attacks, insults, and aggressive behaviour are not welcome.
  • No discrimination: Hate speech, racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, and other forms of discrimination are strictly forbidden.
  • Be mindful of cultural differences: Rheoa is global. What is acceptable in one culture may not be in another. When in doubt, be polite.

2. Safety & Privacy

Your safety and privacy – and that of every other player – is paramount.

  • Do not share personal information: Never share your real name, phone number, email address, home address, bank details, passwords, or OTPs with anyone.
  • Do not ask for personal information: Do not pressure other players to share their personal or financial details.
  • Protect your account: Never share your password or auth tokens. We will never ask for them.
  • Report suspicious behaviour: If someone makes you uncomfortable or attempts to scam you, stop the conversation immediately and report them using the in‑game report function.

3. Prohibited Content & Behaviour

The following activities are not allowed anywhere on Rheoa (chat, forums, profiles, gang messages, testimonials, or any other user‑generated content):

  • Harassment & Bullying: Targeting individuals with abusive, threatening, or unwanted messages.
  • Hate Speech: Content that attacks or demeans based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or any other protected characteristic.
  • Explicit or NSFW Content: Pornography, sexually explicit material, gore, or extreme violence.
  • Impersonation: Pretending to be another player, a staff member, or a public figure.
  • Doxxing: Sharing another person’s real‑world personal information without their explicit consent.
  • Spam & Disruption: Flooding chat, posting repetitive messages, or disrupting gameplay for others.
  • Scams & Fraud: Attempting to deceive other players for personal gain (e.g., fake trades, fake freelance orders, phishing).
  • Exploits & Cheating: Using bugs, glitches, bots, or unauthorised third‑party tools to gain an unfair advantage.
  • Real‑Money Trading (RMT): Buying or selling virtual currency, items, or accounts for real money outside of the official freelance marketplace (which uses escrow).
  • Illegal Activities: Any content that promotes or facilitates illegal activities under local or international law.

4. Moderation & Consequences

We reserve the right to take action against any player who violates these guidelines. The severity of the consequence will match the severity and frequency of the violation.

⚠️ Warning
First‑time or minor offences. You will receive a formal warning via in‑game notification. No account restrictions are applied, but repeat offences will escalate.
⏳ Timeout / Mute
Chat or forum spam, minor harassment. Your ability to send messages in chat or post in forums may be temporarily suspended for a set period (e.g., 1 hour to 24 hours).
🚫 Temporary Ban
Serious or repeat violations. Your account will be suspended for a set period (24 hours, 7 days, or 30 days). You will not be able to log in or play during this time.
⛔ Permanent Ban
Extreme or repeated severe violations. Your account will be permanently closed. You will lose all virtual items, currency, and progress. This decision is final.
🛡️ Moderator Discretion: Our moderation team (Admins and Founders) has full discretion to apply the appropriate consequence, considering the context and history of the user. We may also use automated tools to detect spam, hate speech, or other violations.

5. Reporting Violations

If you encounter a player who is violating these guidelines, we encourage you to report them. Your reports help us keep the community safe.

  • In‑game Report: In chat or DMs, you can report a user by clicking the “Report” button (available in DM windows and the chat interface).
  • Contact a Moderator: Founders and moderators are available in the game. You can reach out to them directly.
  • Email: For serious issues, you can email us at support@rheoa.com with a detailed description and any screenshots.

We take all reports seriously and will investigate them promptly. We may not always disclose the outcome of an investigation, but we will take appropriate action where needed.

6. Appeals

If you believe a moderation action was taken against you in error, you have the right to appeal. To submit an appeal:

  • Email: Send an email to appeals@rheoa.com with your username, the action taken, and the reason you believe it was a mistake.
  • Be patient: We will review your case and respond within 7–10 business days.
  • Decision: The moderation team’s decision on appeals is final. Repeated frivolous appeals may result in additional restrictions.

7. Guidelines for Specific Features

💬 Chat & Direct Messages

  • Keep conversations respectful. If someone asks you to stop a topic, please respect that.
  • Do not spam, advertise, or post unsolicited links (except in the designated sharing areas).
  • Use the “Snooze” or “Block” feature if someone is bothering you, and report them if necessary.

📝 Forums & Comments

  • Stay on topic. Off‑topic posts may be removed.
  • Constructive criticism is welcome, but trolling or deliberate provocation is not.
  • Content that is irrelevant, spammy, or low‑quality may be removed.

🏢 Gangs

  • Gang names, descriptions, and logos must also comply with these guidelines.
  • Gang‑based harassment or rivalry is allowed as role‑play, but must not cross into real harassment or abuse.

📜 Testimonials & Profiles

  • Scrapbook (testimonial) entries should be kind or constructive. Negative testimonials targeting individuals may be removed.
  • Do not post false or misleading information about other players.

8. Updates to These Guidelines

We may update these Community Guidelines from time to time to reflect changes in the game, community needs, or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes via in‑game notifications or by updating the “Last updated” date above.

Your continued use of Rheoa after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated guidelines. If you do not agree, you should stop using the platform.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about these guidelines or need to report a serious issue, please reach out to us:

  • General Support: support@rheoa.com
  • Appeals: appeals@rheoa.com
  • In‑game: Contact a Moderator or Founder via chat.

Thank you for doing your part to make Rheoa a great place to play. See you in the game! 🎮
