🌱 How to Garden

  1. Buy Seeds: Purchase seeds from the shop below using your cash.
  2. Plant: Click the "Plant" button on an empty plot, then select a seed from the popup.
  3. Water: Click "Water" when the plant is dry (it will show "Wilted" status). Watering also advances growth stages.
  4. Fertilize & Spray: Use Fertilize and Pest Spray to increase your yield (optional, costs energy).
  5. Harvest: When ready (Harvestable status), click "Harvest" to collect crops and gain XP.
  6. Clear Dead: If a plant dies (no water for too long), click "Clear" to remove it and reuse the plot.
  7. Express Grow (Black+): Instantly grow a plant for cash.
  8. Upgrade Plot (Black+): Upgrade a plot to "Irrigated" for faster growth.

💡 Hint: Keep an eye on the progress bar and water regularly to prevent wilting!
