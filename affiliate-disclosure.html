🛒 Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: 13 July 2026 · Rheoa RPG

Rheoa RPG (“we”, “us”, or “our”) believes in full transparency. To keep the game free and sustainable, we participate in several affiliate marketing programs. This means that when you click on certain links on our platform and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission – at no extra cost to you.

This disclosure explains how affiliate links work on Rheoa, which programs we participate in, and what it means for you as a visitor anywhere in the world.

🍪 Transparency First: We only recommend products and services that we genuinely believe add value to our community. Our editorial integrity is never influenced by affiliate commissions.

1. Amazon Associates Program

Rheoa is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com, Amazon.co.uk, Amazon.de, Amazon.in, and other international Amazon stores.

As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases. This applies to:

  • Hot Wheels Collectibles – when you click “Collect Now” on our Racing page, you are taken to Amazon where you can purchase the item.
  • Other recommended products – we may occasionally link to other relevant items that we believe you will enjoy.

Important: The price you pay on Amazon is exactly the same whether you use our link or go directly to Amazon. The commission we earn comes from Amazon’s marketing budget, not from your wallet.

2. Impact & Other Affiliate Networks

In addition to Amazon, we work with other trusted affiliate networks such as Impact (formerly Impact Radius), Rakuten, and similar platforms. These networks enable us to partner with a wide range of brands and offer you relevant products and services.

  • How they work: When you click an affiliate link, a cookie may be placed on your browser to track the referral. This helps the network attribute the sale to us.
  • What we earn: Commissions vary by program and product, but typically range from 1% to 10% of the purchase price.
  • No extra cost: As with Amazon, using our affiliate links does not increase the price you pay.

We use these networks because they allow us to offer a broader range of products across different regions, ensuring that relevant offers are shown to visitors in the US, UK, Europe, India, and beyond.

3. Global Visitors & Geo‑Targeting

Rheoa attracts players from all over the world. Our affiliate links are designed to respect your location and provide the best possible experience:

  • Geo‑redirects: When you click an Amazon affiliate link, you may be automatically redirected to your local Amazon store (e.g., amazon.co.uk for the UK, amazon.de for Germany, amazon.in for India, etc.).
  • Localised offers: The products and prices you see are specific to your region, ensuring relevance and availability.
  • Regional commissions: Commissions vary by region. For example, Amazon US may offer different rates than Amazon UK. This does not affect your purchase.
🌍 Global Compliance: We ensure that all affiliate links comply with local advertising and privacy laws in the regions we serve, including GDPR (EU/UK), CCPA (California), and similar regulations worldwide.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Like most affiliate programs, our links may use cookies to track the referral and ensure we receive proper credit for the sale. These cookies are:

  • Third‑party cookies – set by Amazon, Impact, or other networks.
  • Limited duration – typically lasting 24 hours to 90 days, depending on the program.
  • Privacy‑friendly – they do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email, or address.

You can manage or disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. For more details, please see our Cookie Policy.

5. Our Commitment to You

We take our responsibility to our community seriously. Here is what we promise:

  • Honest Recommendations: We only link to products or services we genuinely believe are useful, fun, or valuable to our players.
  • Clear Labelling: Affiliate links on Rheoa are clearly marked (e.g., “Collect Now” or “Shop”), so you know when you are clicking one.
  • No Hidden Costs: Using our affiliate links never increases the price you pay. You pay exactly what you would pay if you went directly to the store.
  • Data Protection: We do not collect or store any personal data from affiliate transactions. All purchase data is handled solely by the affiliate network or the retailer (e.g., Amazon).
