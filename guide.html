📖 The Basics

Welcome to Rheoa RPG, a text-based criminal empire simulator! Your goal is to build wealth, gain experience, and become the most notorious criminal mastermind. Start small and work your way up!

🎯 Getting Started

💰
Start with Jobs
Work legitimate jobs to build your initial capital safely. No risk, steady income.
🔫
Move to Crimes
Once you have cash, try crimes for bigger rewards. Higher risk = higher reward!
⚡
Manage Energy
Every action costs energy. It regenerates 1 point per minute automatically.
⭐
Level Up
Gain XP from jobs and crimes to level up and unlock better opportunities.

🎯 Core Gameplay Loop

  1. Work Jobs - Safe but lower rewards
  2. Commit Crimes - Risky but higher rewards
  3. Earn Cash & XP - Build your wealth and experience
  4. Buy Items - Purchase helpful items from the shop
  5. Level Up - Unlock new crimes and jobs
  6. Repeat - Aim for higher levels and bigger crimes

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get more energy? +
Energy regenerates automatically at a rate of 1 point per minute. You can also use Energy Drink items to restore energy instantly. The energy bar shows a timer for when your next energy point will regenerate.
What happens when I fail a crime? +
When you fail a crime, you lose the fail penalty amount from your cash and your health decreases. You still lose the energy cost. Make sure to have enough cash buffer and health items before attempting risky crimes!
How do I increase my success rate? +
Success rate can be increased by:
- Using temporary boost items (Energy Drink, Lucky Charm)
- Permanent boosts from achievements
- Training Manual items for permanent boost
- Higher level also unlocks easier crimes
Why are some crimes/jobs locked? +
Crimes and jobs have minimum level requirements. Check the level badge on each card. You need to reach that level by gaining XP from easier activities first.
How does stat decay work? +
Hunger and happiness decrease by 5 points for every hour you're away from the game. When you log back in, the game calculates how many hours have passed and reduces your stats accordingly. Keep them balanced with food and entertainment items!
What are achievements? +
Achievements are milestones you can complete, like committing 100 crimes or earning ₹1,000,000. When you complete them, you earn cash and XP rewards. Check the Achievements page to track your progress!
Can I play on mobile? +
Yes! The game is fully responsive and works on all devices. The interface adapts to your screen size for the best experience on phone, tablet, or desktop.

💡 Pro Tips & Strategies

💰
Start with jobs - Build a cash buffer before attempting crimes. This protects you from bankruptcy on failed attempts.
⚡
Use energy wisely - Don't waste energy on low-reward activities when you're close to leveling up. Save it for better opportunities.
🍽️
Keep stats balanced - Low hunger or happiness can affect your performance. Always keep them above 50% for optimal results.
📊
Track success rates - Different crimes have different base success rates. Pick ones that match your risk tolerance.
🏆
Check achievements - Regularly check your achievements page. You might be close to completing some without realizing it!
⏰
Log in regularly - Energy regenerates and stats decay over time. Logging in every few hours helps you stay on top of your stats.
🎒
Stock up on items - Buy items in bulk when you have extra cash. You never know when you'll need a quick health restore or energy boost.
📈
Plan your progression - Aim for specific level milestones. Each new tier unlocks significantly better rewards.
🍀
Save boost items - Use temporary boost items before attempting high-level crimes for maximum benefit.
📜
Learn from history - Check your crime history to see which crimes you succeed at most often. Focus on your strengths!
