📖 The Basics

Welcome to Rheoa RPG, a text-based criminal empire simulator! Your goal is to build wealth, gain experience, and become the most notorious criminal mastermind. Start small and work your way up!

🎯 Getting Started

💰
Start with Jobs
Work legitimate jobs to build your initial capital safely. No risk, steady income.
🔫
Move to Crimes
Once you have cash, try crimes for bigger rewards. Higher risk = higher reward!
⚡
Manage Energy
Every action costs energy. It regenerates 1 point per minute automatically.
⭐
Level Up
Gain XP from jobs and crimes to level up and unlock better opportunities.

🎯 Core Gameplay Loop

  1. Work Jobs - Safe but lower rewards
  2. Commit Crimes - Risky but higher rewards
  3. Earn Cash & XP - Build your wealth and experience
  4. Buy Items - Purchase helpful items from the shop

  7. Battle in PvP - Fight other players for loot
  8. Trade on Market - Buy and sell items with players
  9. Invest in Stocks - Play the stock market for profit
  10. Race in Competitions - Win races and earn prizes
  11. Buy Property - Own and upgrade your criminal headquarters
  12. Customize Your Profile - Unlock titles, avatars, and cosmetics
  13. Explore the World - Discover new regions and secrets
  14. Collect Daily Rewards - Login every day for bonuses
  15. Prestige - Reset your progress for permanent power

⚔️ Player vs Player Combat

Challenge other players to battles and prove your superiority!

Battle Mechanics

  • Energy Cost - 20 energy per battle
  • Health Loss - Both players lose 10 health
  • Loot - Winner takes 5% of defender's cash
  • XP Reward - 50 XP for participating
  • Cooldown - Can't attack same player for 1 hour

Ranking System

  • Rank Points - Start at 1000, gain/lose based on opponent strength
  • Rank Titles - Rookie → Scrapper → Brawler → Fighter → Warrior → Gladiator → Champion → Master → Grandmaster → Legend → Mythic → Godlike
  • Win/Loss Record - Tracks your PvP performance
  • Streaks - Consecutive wins earn bonus bragging rights

Tips for PvP

  • Check opponent power - Compare health and rank before attacking
  • Save energy - Make sure you have enough energy for the battle
  • Watch cooldowns - Can't farm the same player repeatedly
  • Build a streak - Consecutive wins increase your rank faster

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get more energy? +
Energy regenerates automatically at a rate of 1 point per minute. You can also use Energy Drink items to restore energy instantly. The energy bar shows a timer for when your next energy point will regenerate. Prestige perks can increase your energy regeneration rate.
What happens when I fail a crime? +
When you fail a crime, you lose the fail penalty amount from your cash and your health decreases. You still lose the energy cost. Make sure to have enough cash buffer and health items before attempting risky crimes!
How do I increase my success rate? +
Success rate can be increased by:
- Using temporary boost items (Energy Drink, Lucky Charm)
- Permanent boosts from achievements
- Training Manual items for permanent boost
- Prestige perks (Crime Genius)
- Property features (Shooting Range)
- Higher level also unlocks easier crimes
Why are some crimes/jobs locked? +
Crimes and jobs have minimum level requirements. Check the level badge on each card. You need to reach that level by gaining XP from easier activities first. Some may also be region-locked until you discover certain areas.
How does stat decay work? +
Hunger and happiness decrease by 5 points for every hour you're away from the game. When you log back in, the game calculates how many hours have passed and reduces your stats accordingly. Keep them balanced with food and entertainment items!
What are achievements? +
Achievements are milestones you can complete, like committing 100 crimes or earning ₹1,000,000. When you complete them, you earn cash and XP rewards. Check the Achievements page to track your progress!
How does PvP work? +
PvP battles cost 20 energy. Both players lose 10 health. The winner takes 5% of the defender's cash and earns 50 XP. You can't attack the same player for 1 hour. Your rank points determine your title and matchmaking.
What are the benefits of joining a gang? +
Gangs provide permanent passive bonuses to all members, including XP boosts, cash boosts, energy regeneration, crime success rate, PvP power, and market fee reductions. You also get access to gang chat and can participate in gang activities.
How does the market work? +
The market allows you to buy and sell items with other players. Listings cost a 5% fee and last 7 days. You can buy any quantity from listings. Price history helps you determine fair prices.
How do stocks work? +
The stock market lets you invest in companies. You need to transfer cash to your trading balance first. Stock prices change based on volatility and market news. Some stocks pay dividends. You can track your portfolio value and profit/loss.
How does racing work? +
First, buy a vehicle from the shop. Each vehicle has different stats (speed, acceleration, handling, nitro). Join races that match your level. Race outcomes depend on your vehicle stats and a bit of luck. Winners earn cash from the prize pool.
What can I do with a property? +
Properties let you store cash safely away from your main wallet. You can upgrade security to prevent robberies, increase storage capacity, and add special features like safe rooms, shooting ranges, and drug labs for passive income. Other players can try to rob you!
How do I unlock customization items? +
Titles, avatars, frames, and color schemes are unlocked through achievements, prestige levels, special events, or by purchasing them with in-game cash. Once unlocked, you can equip them from the Customization page to personalize your profile.
What is the world map for? +
The world map lets you explore different regions. Each region has its own unique crimes, jobs, and items. You can discover hidden secrets, find special caches, and unlock new areas as you level up. Travel between regions costs cash and time.
What are daily rewards? +
Daily rewards give you free items and resources every day you log in. Your streak increases with consecutive logins, and every 7 days you get a 2x bonus. There are also special monthly rewards on holidays!
What is Prestige mode? +
Prestige mode becomes available at level 10. When you prestige, you reset your level, XP, cash, and items, but earn Prestige Points based on your accomplishments. These points can be spent on permanent perks that boost all your future gameplay. Each prestige level makes you permanently stronger!
Can I play on mobile? +
Yes! The game is fully responsive and works on all devices. The interface adapts to your screen size for the best experience on phone, tablet, or desktop. The map is especially designed for touch interaction on mobile.
