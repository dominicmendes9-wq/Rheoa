🏰 Welcome to Conquest!

Goal: Conquer the world by taking over regions.

How to play:

  • Click any gray region to claim it (if unowned).
  • Click a green region (yours) to set it as your attack base.
  • Then click a red region (NPC) to conquer it.
  • Use the End Turn button to advance time and let NPCs grow.
